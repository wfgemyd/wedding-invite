Gifts & Flowers

A small request, with love.

With all due respect

Your presence is the greatest gift to us. Please, no boxed gifts.

If you would like to give something, we would truly appreciate a small contribution in cash to support our young family and future plans.

The same goes for bouquets: instead of many separate arrangements, we would prefer the “flowers” to be a cash contribution.

And if you still feel like bringing a flower, that would be wonderful — we will gather all the flowers from our guests into one big bouquet.

It will be our favorite bouquet of all: made from the people we love, who came to celebrate with us.

Our story

A bit about us

How it started

It started with a Tinder match and a first date at Náplavka—easy laughs, river air, and zero awkwardness. Their first real conversation somehow turned into oatmeal dishes, and Daniel’s homemade oatmeal cookies stole the show. Oksana had already seen him baking them on Instagram… and that was the moment her crush turned into something real. And now, two years later, they’re celebrating their anniversary in the best way possible: by getting married. Two years full of challenges, fun, love, and adventures—plus an endless collection of oatmeal recipes along the way.
