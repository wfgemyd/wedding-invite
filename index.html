How it started

It began with a random Tinder match—one cute date, a lot of laughter, and that “this feels different” kind of spark. Then Oksana left for Turkey, and instead of fading, the connection grew even stronger from afar. When she returned, Daniel met her at the airport, and from that moment, the two of them were all in. Little by little, Oksana’s visits turned into staying over… and “Daniel’s place” quietly became “their place.” Life tested them with two tough shoulder surgeries, and Oksana stayed right there beside Daniel through every step of recovery. Not long after, Daniel graduated with his BSc, landed a great job, and together they moved into the home they share today—ready for the next chapter.
